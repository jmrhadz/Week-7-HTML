About Us

We make functional and decorational pottery, ceramic art and watercolor paintings of places and scenes.
While Kate is a full-time art teacher and John Mark is trying to break into the tech industry,
they spend much of their free time throwing pots in their garage-turned-studio or painting and drawing at local cafes.
Kate and John Mark met in college in Springfield, Missouri, where they were taking art classes.
In their first year of marriage, they moved to Japan on a whim and got into the habit of drawing almost every day.
